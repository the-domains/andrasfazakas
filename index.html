Normal pressure hydrocephalus

Dr. Andras Fazakas / YouTube
Summary A type of dementia Cerebrovascular fluid (CSF) accumulates in the brain because the subarachnoid granulations don't absorb the CSF. This leads to ventricular enlargement which can be checked by MRI or CT. 3 symptoms: Magnetic gait, urinary incontinence, dementia. Lumbar puncture leads to improvement in gait, continence, and cognition.

Sjögren's syndrome

Dr. Andras Fazakas / YouTube
Andras Fazakas Sjögren's syndrome A chronic systemic inflammatory autoimmune disease that can cause death if : pulmonary infection, a lymphoma, or renal failure. T & B lymphocytes of the immune system invades the exocrine glands in your body. Eventually this invasion leads to ductal epithelial cell hyperplasia, which cause a duct obstruction.
