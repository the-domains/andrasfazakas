Dr. Andras Fazakas / YouTube
Andras Fazakas Sjögren's syndrome A chronic systemic inflammatory autoimmune disease that can cause death if : pulmonary infection, a lymphoma, or renal failure. T & B lymphocytes of the immune system invades the exocrine glands in your body. Eventually this invasion leads to ductal epithelial cell hyperplasia, which cause a duct obstruction.
